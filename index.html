📖 Gameplay Guide

💓 Life Pulse
Particles expand/contract like a heart. Gesture: Make a fist to trigger rapid heartbeat; open hand to calm down.

🌫️ Noise Drift
Particles flow in a 3D noise field like smoke. Gesture: Your palm is a 'gravity well', attracting particles.

🌊 3D Sine Wave
Adjust X/Y/Z amplitude independently in the right panel for 3D ripples!

🌍 Auto Spin
Enable X/Y/Z auto-rotation in the right panel for a cinematic 3D view.

GESTURE: NONE
